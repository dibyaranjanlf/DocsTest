# Reset Password

If you do not remember your Linux Foundation login password or need to reset it, you can make a request to get your password reset. A password change invalidates any of your CommunityBridge sessions and you must sign in again.

**Do these steps:**

1. On the Sign in page, click **Forgot Password? Result:** The Forgot your password? page appears.
2. Enter your username or email address and click **Reset Password**. ![](https://firebasestorage.googleapis.com/v0/b/gitbook-28427.appspot.com/o/assets%2Flinux-foundation-documentation%2F-M2DD0Aet7mbzEuwibdA%2F-M2DE4FvEw8i4RhigoTC%2F7418504.png?generation=1584008035901308&alt=media)â€‹
3. Check your email inbox for an `CommunityBridge: Reset Password` email and open it.
4. Click **Reset Password**.
5. Enter your new password and confirm it in the Change Password page. Click **Submit**. ![](https://firebasestorage.googleapis.com/v0/b/gitbook-28427.appspot.com/o/assets%2Flinux-foundation-documentation%2F-M2DD0Aet7mbzEuwibdA%2F-M2DE4Ft-_2lQFyVvHDg%2F7418502.png?generation=1584008035918747&alt=media) The status bar confirms that your password is updated.
